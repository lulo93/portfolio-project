About me

Hola! I'm Lu, a british Colombian living in London. I have been working in the Financial Services sector for over 5 years and as much as I have enjoyed my career in Wealth Management it was time for a change. I had developed a burning desire to learn new skills and explore a different field but I wasn't entirely sure where to begin, someone very close to me insisted that Technology was the next best sector to learn about. I had always been curious about Software Development, one day my friend sent me a SheCodes ad on instagram and I haven't looked back since. Now, four months on, I am very close to graduating from the SheCodes Pro course and hopefully achieving my career change in 2022!
